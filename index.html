I am currently a fifth-year PhD candidate in the Mechanical & Aerospace Department at Cornell University. I work with Professor Hadas Kress-Gazit in the Verifiable Robotics Research Group. I am graciously funded by the NDSEG Fellowship.

My resume/CV can be found here.




Research

I am interested in formal methods for robotics, multi-agent coordination, and task and motion planning. My research focuses on how robots can autonomously decide to participate and form teams to perform complex high-level tasks.
